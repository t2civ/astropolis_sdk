# Players (Descriptive)

Players are significant "actors" in the simulation, including spacefaring nations, space agencies, and space companies (the latter not yet added).

Notes:

1. In the simulation, all strata are either "territorial" (belonging to nations) or "commons". Earth continental strata except Antarctica are territorial. This includes continental surface, continental shelf, and continental strata below to the mantle. Atmosphere, ocean, ocean floor, ocean crust, mantle, core, and Antarctic strata are commons, as are all off-Earth strata. (This simplification means that nations own continental shelf but not the ocean above.)
2. A player can be *part of* another player. Specifically, all agencies here are part of a national polity. This means that their "population" and economic activity are counted as part of the national polity's population and activity.
3. Table entity names are derived from short forms (USA, RUSSIA, etc.; use EU for EU+).


## National Polities

- United States of America (USA)
- Russian Federation (Russia)
- People's Republic of China (China)
- European Union Plus (EU+) — EU plus EEA nations, UK, Switzerland, and associated microstates.
- Republic of India (India)
- Japan
- Other — This catchall entity represents the territory and activity of all other nations (includes Greenland).


## Agencies

- National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) — Space agency of USA. Encompasses all NASA centers (JPL, Goddard, etc.) and their associated contractor workforce.
- Roscosmos State Corporation for Space Activities (Roscosmos) — Space agency of Russia. Encompasses the state corporation and its subsidiary space industry enterprises.
- China National Space Administration (CNSA) — Space agency of China. Encompasses all Chinese government-directed space activities, including CASC (China Aerospace Science and Technology Corporation) and CASIC as the industrial base.
- European Space Agency (ESA) — Space agency of EU+ (this is a simulation simplification). Encompasses ESA and EU+ national space agencies (CNES, DLR, ASI, UKSA, etc.) together with the European space industry.
- Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) — Space agency of India. Encompasses ISRO centers and associated entities such as Antrix Corporation and NSIL.
- Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA) — Space agency of Japan. Encompasses JAXA and the Japanese space industry (MHI Space Systems, IHI Aerospace, NEC Space Technologies, MELCO satellite division, etc.).
